# The Science of Deduction **Sherlock Holmes**, the world's only *consulting detective*, resides at 221B Baker Street. Created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Holmes is renowned for his immense observational skills, mastery of disguise, and strict logical reasoning. --- ## Core Methodologies Holmes employs a rigorous, scientific approach to crime-solving alongside his loyal friend and chronicler, Dr. John H. Watson: * **Observation:** Noticing the mundane details others miss (e.g., the specific splash of mud on a trouser leg or the wear on a pocket watch). * **Deduction:** Drawing strict logical conclusions from those exact observations. * **Esoteric Knowledge:** Maintaining a mental database of bizarre facts, such as identifying 140 distinct varieties of tobacco ash. ### A Guiding Philosophy > "When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, *however improbable*, must be the truth." > — *The Sign of the Four* ## Essential Case Files 1. *A Study in Scarlet* (The introduction of Holmes and Watson) 2. *The Hound of the Baskervilles* (A haunting mystery on the moors) 3. *The Adventure of the Speckled Band* (A classic locked-room puzzle) !!!
